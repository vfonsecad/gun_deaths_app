FRAZIER PARK, CALIFORNIA (Friday, Feb. 1, 2013 at 7:35 a.m.)–A man reportedly suffered a gunshot wound on Thursday at about 5:45 p.m., according to Kern County Fire Department Captain Jason Retzlaff, whose fire crew responded to the medical aid call. Retzlaff said the call directed fire crews to a location on Pico Trail in Frazier Park. Captain Retzlaff confirmed a breaking news tip received at The Mountain Enterprise suggesting that the gunshot wound was self-inflicted.
