CHESAPEAKE A 29-year-old man was found shot to death in the 2800 block of Parkside Drive early this morning. Chesapeake police officers were called to the location around 5:50 a.m. and found Torrey Smith of Norfolk with a gunshot wound. He was pronounced dead at the scene, according to police.
