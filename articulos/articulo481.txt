1   						   							  							 Columbia, SC (WLTX) -- Richland County Coroner Gary Watts has identified the man who was shot and killed just after 11pm Friday night on the 4016 block of Williamsburg Drive. Coroner Watts says the victim was 22-year old Brandon Jones of Columbia. Jones died during surgery around 2:15am Saturday morning as a result of complications from gun shot wounds to his upper body.  According to officials, two unknown gunmen forced their way into the residence and fired shots at three people. Deputies say the other two victims were shot in the upper body and were transported to a local hospital. Their conditions are unknown. Deputies are still trying to find a motive for this shooting.  If you know anything about this incident you are urged to call Crime Stopper at 1-888-CRIME-SC.
