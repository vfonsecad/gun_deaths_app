1 1                                                  Tribune photo illustration                                                 (Nancy Stone / Chicago Tribune)                                          9:35 a.m. CDT, August 6, 2013 A 33-year-old St. Louis man died after being shot over the weekend in the backyard of a relative's home in the Archer Heights neighborhood, officials said. Francisco Chairez-Munoz, who police said was visiting relatives was in the yard of a home on the 5100 block of South Kostner Avenue at about 2 a.m. Saturday, said Chicago Police Department News Affairs Officer Jose Estrada. As Chairez-Munoz and the relative were in the yard someone snuck into the yard from an alley and began firing, striking Chairez-Munoz, police said. After the shooting the offender fled south, police said. Chairez-Munoz was initially taken to Advocate Christ Medical Center  where he wsa later pronounced dead , at 11:40 p.m. Monday, according to the Cook County medical examiner's office. Area Central detectives are investigating.
