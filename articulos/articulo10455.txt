1 1 Las Vegas Metro police said they are looking for a man accused of shooting a man to death near downtown Sunday night. The shooting happened at 10:47 p.m. in the 1700 block of North J Street. Police said arriving officers found the body of a man at the scene. They said the man, identified by the Clark County Coroner's Office as 45-year-old Stacey Randolph, had suffered a gunshot wound. Investigators described the shooter as a black man, in his early 20s, between 5'9" and 5'10" tall and 250 to 300 pounds. They said the man has a lazy eye and may be driving a white sedan.
