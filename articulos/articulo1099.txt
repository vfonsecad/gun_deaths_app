Officers responded to the scene on Ardsley Court around 3:30 a.m. and found Matthew Obrian Norris, 28, suffering from several gunshot wounds.  EMS took Norris to Cone Hospital where he later died.  Deputies identified the shooter as 28-year-old<c2><a0>Charles Tyler Forrest, who lived at the scene. Investigators say Forrest is cooperating with the investigation.  No charges have been filed in the case because detectives are still trying to determine if the shooting was in self defense.    							GCSO
