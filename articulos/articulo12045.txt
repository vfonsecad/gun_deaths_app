1 1 NORFOLK Police have a vague description of a getaway vehicle used after a man was shot to death early this morning. Officers responded a few minutes before 1 a.m., to calls of shots fired in the 7900 block of West Glen Road, a police news release said. Those arriving found the man inside a home, suffering from at least one gunshot wound. The man was pronounced dead at the scene. This afternoon, police identified him as 58-year-old Warren P. Fisher. He lived on the block. Police think robbery may be a motive. After talking to witnesses, they said two males fled the scene in a car, going toward East Little Creek Road. They car is thought to be a Nissan Maxima or Altima. Anyone with information on the incident is asked to call the Crime Line at 1-888-LOCK-U-UP.
