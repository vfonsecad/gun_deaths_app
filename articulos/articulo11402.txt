1 1 An East Atlanta woman faces a murder charge after fatally shooting a man inside her Clifton Road home early Friday. Victoria Rickman, 30, is charged with murder and felony murder in the death of William Carter, Jr., 30, of Marietta. The cause of death is homicide due to multiple gunshot wounds. According to Atlanta police, officers responded to a call of a person shot at 2:30 a.m. in the 800 block of Clifton Road in southeast Atlanta. When police arrived, they found the man dead with several gunshot wounds.
