1 1 Jackson police are investigating a double shooting that occurred Friday night near Medgar Evers Boulevard.  The shooting happened just after 11 p.m. Friday at the Commonwealth Village apartments on Skyview Drive. Hinds County Coroner Sharon Grisham Stewart has identified the victim as 56-year-old Robert Finch.According to JPD officials, Finch was pronounced dead on the scene, after being shot multiple times.  Another person was shot in the head, chest and arm. This person is listed in critical condition. At this time, no motive has been released. Copyright 2013 MSNewsNow.
