LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Police found Qualisha Mitchell, 26, suffering from a gunshot wound to the head on Natural Bridge near Belt Avenue on Thursday at around 11pm.  She was taken to the hospital but has died as a result of her injuries. The victims were traveling westbound on Natural Bridge when a white Chevy Malibu pulled up next to their car. The unknown suspects then opened fire at the victim’s vehicle. Their vehicle was last seen traveling westbound on Natural Bridge. The three other people in the car with her were not injured.
