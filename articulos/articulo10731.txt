1 1 A 16-year-old has been shot and killed overnight on the 1100 block of Logan Street on Saginaw's east side. The shooting happened around 2 a.m. Tuesday morning. The area is between Williamson and Washington streets near Sheridan Avenue. The victim has been identified as Stone Maeem Roberson. He was pronounced dead at the scene. Many shell casings were scattered about the area. Roberson was a student at Saginaw Arthur Hill High School. Police do not have any suspects in custody in the shooting death. Saginaw Police and Michigan State Police are continuing to investigate. Anyone with information is urged to contact Crimestoppers at 1-800-422-JAIL.
