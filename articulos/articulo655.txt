Police responded to a report of a shooting in the 1100 block of  32nd Street at 6:09 p.m., Officer J. Moore said. When officers arrived, they found two men suffering from gunshot  wounds, Moore said. 	   Both men, whose identities were not yet released, were transported  to a hospital. One was pronounced dead and the other is in critical but  stable condition, Moore said. 	   Police are investigating the shooting, and are searching for two  armed suspects in connection with the incident. Earlier in the evening, police responded to a report of a shooting  in the 700 block of 25th Street at 4:38 p.m., Moore said. Officers found a man suffering from a gunshot wound and  transported him to a hospital, Moore said. He is in stable condition. The suspect in the shooting remains at large.
