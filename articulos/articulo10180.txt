1 1 YORKTOWN Police have identified the victim and the suspect in what appears to be a murder-suicide. Mildred F. Baldwin, 74, and her son-in law Marvin J. Bowen, 53, were found shot to death Thursday in the driveway of a home in the 100 block of Runaway Lane, according to the York-Poquoson Sheriff's Office. Deputies were called to the home about 2 p.m.   In a news release, the Sheriff's Office said it appeared that the man shot and killed the woman before turning the gun on himself.
