1 UPDATE: Composite  Sketch in Fatal Shooting at 301 West Little  York Houston police have released a composite sketch  of a suspect wanted in the fatal shooting of a man at 301 West Little York about  12:30 a.m. on January 16.  The suspect is described only as a Hispanic male  in his late 20s, about 5 feet 9 inches tall, with a slightly heavy build.  A  composite sketch of the suspect, who goes by the nickname of "El Grande," is  attached to this news release.  The victim, Felipe Juarez, 34, of Houston, was  transported to Ben Taub General Hospital in critical condition.  Doctors  pronounced him dead on January 21.  HPD Homicide Division Sergeant J. Rhoads and  Senior Police Officer R. Bolton reported:  Mr. Juarez went to an apartment complex at the  above address to visit some friends.  While he was in the parking lot, Juarez  and his group were stopped by five unknown male suspects armed with guns.  The  group was led back up to the apartment where Juarez's friends live.  The  suspects ransacked and then left the apartment.  Juarez left the apartment and,  as he walked to the bottom of the stairs, he was shot by one or more suspects.   Paramedics were called and transported Juarez to the hospital.  The suspects are described only as three black  and two Hispanic males.  They were last seen in a black 2000 to 2006 Chevrolet  Tahoe.  A photo of a similar vehicle is attached to this news  release.  Anyone with information in this case is urged to  contact the HPD Homicide Division at 713-308-3600 or Crime Stoppers at  713-222-TIPS.
