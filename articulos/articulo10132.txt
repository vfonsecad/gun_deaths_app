The victim has been identified as 32-year-old Miguel Garcia Sanchez. No suspects have been arrested, police say. The shooting apparently took place shortly before 10:30 p.m. in the 1100 block of West Sugar Creek Road, not far from Reagan Drive and Interstate 85. Investigators say Sanchez and a friend were sitting in their car in a gas station parking lot when they were approached by a man who pulled a gun and demanded money. During the attempted robbery, Sanchez was shot. The gunman fled. The getaway vehicle initially was described as a dark-colored sedan. Sanchez was taken by Medic to Carolinas Medical Center, but he died overnight from his injuries. Homicide detectives spent much the night scouring the area near the shooting.
