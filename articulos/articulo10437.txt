1 1 Police have released the identity of Saturday's homicide victim in south Richmond. It happened in the parking lot of the Richmond Inn Motel in the 6300 block of Midlothian Turnpike near Old Warwick Road. Zekei Johnson, 18, of the 300 block of Red Ash Drive was found shot, around 5:30 p.m. Saturday. Johnson died on the scene in the parking lot. It appears he may have been involved in some sort of domestic dispute, but investigators need more information. If you know anything that can help investigators, call Richmond police.
