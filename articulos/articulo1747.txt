Salvador Ponce, of the 2700 block of South Komensky Avenue, was declared dead at 1:45 p.m. at Mount Sinai Hospital after being shot Sunday afternoon, according to the Cook County medical examiner's office. The victim was on the street in the 4100 block of West 30th Street--near Kedvale Avenue, about 4:30 p.m. Sunday when a green minivan drove up and a man or boy jumped out and shot the man in the head, said Chicago Police news Affairs Officer Daniel O'Brien.
