1 1 VIRGINIA BEACH A man was fatally shot late Friday on Coach Drive, police said. The shooting occurred a few minutes after 11 p.m. in the 5400 block of Coach Drive, a police news release said. Jamel Alexander Knight, 23, of Virginia Beach was pronounced dead at the scene, police said. He was attending an event, according to a police news release issued Sunday, and while standing outside he was approached by two people. One of them had a handgun and shot Knight.
