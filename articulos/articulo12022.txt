1 1 FILE — NYPD car is viewed in Times Square. (Photo by Spencer Platt/Getty Images)  NEW YORK (CBSNewYork) — The bodies of two slain men were found overnight in different locations in Queens. The first discovery happened around 1:35 a.m. outside 184-09 140th Ave. in the Rochdale section of Queens. Upon arriving at the scene, police found a 29-year-old man lying in front of a residence with five gunshot wounds to the body. The man, whose identity has not been released, was taken to Jamaica Hospital where he was pronounced dead. Around 4:10 a.m., police found another man lying with multiple gunshot wounds outside the Perfection strip club, at 62-05 30th Ave. between the Astoria and Jackson Heights sections of Queens. The unidentified man, who was reported to be in his 20s, was pronounced dead a short time later at Elmhurst Hospital. There were no arrests in either case as of late Saturday morning, and the investigations continued.
