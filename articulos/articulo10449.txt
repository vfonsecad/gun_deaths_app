1 1 A man who threatened to kill police officers during a standoff in Ambridge was found dead from self-inflicted gunshot wounds, according to Ambridge police. Chief James Mann said Robert Burke, 37, of Ambridge was found at about 8 p.m shot inside of his home at 1216 Duss Avenue. Police were called to the home after 3 p.m by Mr. Burke's girlfriend, who said he sent her text messages threatening to kill himself. He later sent messages threatening to kill police officers before killing himself. Police and first responders evacuated Duss Avenue between 11th and 14th streets from 4 p.m. until after they found Mr. Burke in the evening. Mr. Burke had several weapons in the home, including a high-powered rifle. No one else was hurt in the incident. Deborah M. Todd: dtodd@post-gazette.com or 412-263-1652.
