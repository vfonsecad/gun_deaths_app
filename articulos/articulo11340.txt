1 1                                                  Tribune photo illustration                                                 (Nancy Stone / Chicago Tribune)                                          5:54 a.m. CDT, September 12, 2013 A 19-year-old man was killed and a 20-year-old man wounded in a drive-by attack in the South Deering neighborhood overnight, and two others were wounded in shootings across the South Side.  The fatal shooting happened about 10:53 p.m. in the 10200 block of South Oglesby Avenue. Someone in a vehicle pulled up to a sidewalk where the 19-year-old and 20-year-old were standing and started shooting, police said.  The younger of the two, Antonio Calhoun, of the 1400 block of East 68th Street, was pronounced dead at the scene. The older man with him was shot in the leg and is in good condition at Advocate Trinity Hospital, police said. About an hour earlier, someone shouting gang slogans out of a car shot a 27-year-old man near 53rd Street and Kedzie Avenue in the Gage Park neighborhood. The man was taken to Mount Sinai Hospital with a hip wound, police said.   A 23-year-old woman was hospitalized this evening after being shot in the Fernwood neighborhood, police said. The woman was shot on the 10100 block of South Emerald Avenue, said Chicago Police News Affairs Officer Daniel O'Brien. The woman was shot in the leg and was taken to Advocate Christ Medical Center in good condition, O'Brien said.
