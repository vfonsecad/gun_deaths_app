Police were called to a shooting at the Pressley Road Park in the1400 block of Pressley Road around 1 a.m.   When officers arrived on scene they located a man suffering from an apparent gunshot wound.  The victim, 18-year-old Kwamne Donqurius Clyburn was pronounced dead on scene at around 1:15 a.m., police said. Detectives with the Homicide Unit canvassed the neighborhood to determine whether were are any witnesses to this incident.
