ALERT: Boil your water in Petersburg		 	 Posted on: 12:20 pm, September 13, 2013, by Alix Bryan David Dupree has been charged in the homicide of Danyale Herring.  PHOTO: Richmond Police RICHMOND, Va. (WTVR)–Early Sunday, just after midnight, Richmond Police officers responded to a call for random gunfire off Broad Rock Boulevard. Police found Danyale K. Herring, 24, inside a residence, suffering from life-threatening gunshot wounds.  He was taken to the hospital, but passed away almost 24 hours later. Friday police announced that they had David Dupree, 40, of the 2200 block of North 28th Street, in custody. He has been charged with conspiracy to commit murder in the death of Herring.
