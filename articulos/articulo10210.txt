1 1 (WMC-TV) - A 17-year-old was shot and killed Friday night after several people were fighting in a park, according to Memphis police.  Officers responded to a shooting call at 3975 Bertram Cove where they found the teen with a gunshot wound. He was pronounced dead on the scene. Shots were fired in a nearby park after several people were fighting, according to the preliminary investigation. There is no suspect information available.
