1 1 KNOXVILLE (WATE) - Police are investigating after a man was found shot to death behind a Knoxville Boys & Girls Club. Police say they were responding to reports of shots being fired behind the Boys & Girls Club of America on McConnell Street around 8:00 p.m. on Friday. After searching the area, officers found the body of a man in his fifties. The investigation is ongoing. The victim's identity has not been released.
