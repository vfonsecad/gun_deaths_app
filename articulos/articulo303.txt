Police responded to multiple phone calls of a shooting in the 800 block of North Figueroa Street at 11:40 p.m. Saturday, police Sgt. Phil Craft said. Ruben Gonzalez Jr. of Santa Ana was found shot in the chest and taken to a hospital, where he died, police said. The police gang unit is investigating, Craft said. Anyone with information has been asked to call the Santa Ana Police Department at (714) 245-8648.
