The homeowner, Jacqueline Vargas-Regalado, lives on the second floor and was not injured, said a neighbor. She said the couple living on the first floor were Vargas-Regalado’s parents, but authorities would not confirm who lived there. Police closed Byron Place for much of Tuesday afternoon.
