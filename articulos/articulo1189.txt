1 HAMPTON A man was found shot to death Friday evening in the 600 block of Homestead Ave. after a 911 call reported shots fired in the area. Police this morning identified the victim as James Terrell Williams, a 24-year-old Hampton man. Authorities were called to the area shortly before 8 p.m. Friday and found Williams with a gunshot wound to his upper torso, said Sgt. Jason Price, a Hampton police spokesman. He died at the scene. The motive for the shooting is still under investigation, according to a Hampton police press release. No suspect has been named.
