1 1 Police are now investigating a homicide in South Richmond. Police tell us around 9:23 p.m. Friday they responded to a call for random gunfire in the 600 block of Warwick Village Road, that's at the Woodbriar Apartments. They arrived within minutes and found two men with life-threatening gunshot wounds. Both were taken to VCU Medical Center. 50 year old Angel Hernandez died from his injuries. The second victim, a 31 year old man is still in critical condition. According to witnesses, the suspects are two black males, who were last seen running south behind the 600 block of Warwick Village Drive toward Effingham Street.
