1 By FOX 2 News StaffDETROIT (WJBK) -- Police say the body of a man wrapped in plastic was found in the basement of a home on Detroit's west side.Tips led police to the abandoned home on the 3400 block of Atkinson Thursday afternoon where they made the discovery shortly after 4 p.m. Autopsy results Friday show the African American male victim died from a gunshot wound to the upper back, according to sources.  His identity has not been released.  Anyone with information is asked to Detroit police.
