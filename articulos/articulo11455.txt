1 1 NORFOLK A 38-year-old man died at a hospital after being found shot Sunday night in a residence in the 600 block of W. 29th St. Police were called shortly after 10:30 p.m., according to a news release from spokeswoman Karen Parker-Chesson. Maurice Lamont Mitchell, who lived in the 600 block of W. 29th St., was taken to Sentara Norfolk General Hospital, where he died. Police had no information on a suspect or motive.
