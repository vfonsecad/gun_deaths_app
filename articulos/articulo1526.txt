1 1 PHILADELPHIA (CBS) – Police say one man is dead after he was shot multiple times Sunday afternoon in North Philadelphia. The shooting happened around 3:30 p.m. in the roadway at the intersection of 5th and West Cambria Streets. The 36-year-old victim was taken to Temple Hospital where he died a short time later. No arrests have been made.
