1                                SPRINGFIELD, Mass. (AP) — Springfield police have released the identity of the city’s second homicide victim of the year. Police received a call for shots fired just before 6 a.m. Wednesday. Responding officers found an unresponsive 42-year-old Jimmie Acevedo on the ground outside suffering from multiple gunshots wounds. He was taken by ambulance to Baystate Medical Center where he was pronounced dead about a half hour later. Police said Acevedo was found less than a mile from his home. Nearby residents reported hearing several shots. The shooting remains under investigation and there was no word on suspects.   			Get today's Boston.com headlines sent to your inbox every morning.
