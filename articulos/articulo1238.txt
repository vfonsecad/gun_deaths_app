1 NEWPORT NEWS A 27-year-old woman was shot and killed Saturday night at her apartment complex, police said.  Phyllis Felicia Vinston was pronounced dead at the scene at the Marshall Courts Apartments, located in the 800 block of 35th Street. Police received a call for a shooting shortly before 7 p.m. and found Vinston in the courtyard of the apartment complex suffering from a gunshot wound, according to a news release.  Several gunshots were fired at a different location nearby by one or two male suspects, police said. It was not clear Saturday night if Vinston was the intended target of the shooting, according to the release.  Anyone with information is asked to call Newport News Crime Line at 1-888-LOCK-U-UP.
