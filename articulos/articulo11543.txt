1 1 Lauderhill Police are searching for the person who shot and killed a man Wednesday.Devon Hayles was gunned down at 4065 NW 19th St. around 6:45 p.m., police said. He died at a hospital. Detectives are also seeking witnesses to the shooting. They are circulating a Broward Crime Stoppers flier that shows the victim’s picture and gives details about where and when the shooting occurred.   Investigators are asking anyone with any information about the case to call Detective Atina Johnson at 954-497-4700 or Broward Crime Stoppers at 954-493-8477 or go to  browardcrimestoppers.org. A reward of $3,000 is being offered for information leading to an arrest.
