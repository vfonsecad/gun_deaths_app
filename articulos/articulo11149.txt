1 1 Credit: CW31 STOCKTON (CBS13) – Stockton police say one man was fatally shot during an attempted home invasion robbery overnight. The incident happened at a residence in the 1600 block of Julian Street at around 5:45 a.m., say police. Residents living nearby reported hearing multiple shots. When police arrived, they found the 24-year-old man dead from a gunshot. Homicide investigators took over the investigation. Multiple suspects were involved in the incident. The investigation is ongoing. Stockton police are encouraging citizens to call (209) 937-8377 if they have information about this investigation. They can also call the investigations unit at (209) 937-8323, or Crime Stoppers at (209) 946-0600. Callers can remain anonymous.
