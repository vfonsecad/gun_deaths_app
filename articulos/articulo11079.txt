1 1   						   							  							 ST. LOUIS, Mo. (KSDK) -- St. Louis police are looking into a shooting that killed one and left another injured in downtown Saturday morning. The shooting happened around 12:45 a.m. near the intersection of 3rd and Washington. A car dropped the man and woman off at a local hospital then took off.  The man, identified as Desmond Adair, 21, of the 7000 block of Dawson, died shortly afterward. The woman, 23, is in critical, but stable condition. Adair was shot in the neck. The woman suffered a gunshot wound to the arm. Police say the victims were traveling in the area when suspects in a dark-colored sedan began shooting at them. The victims' vehicle crashed into a light pole after the shooting.   							KSDK
