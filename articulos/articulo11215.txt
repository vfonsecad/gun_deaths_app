1 1          An intruder who was shot when police said he and another person kicked in the door of a University City home died Friday morning at Carolinas Medical Center.  Jamal Antonio Haynes, 17, was taken to the hospital at about 6:45 p.m. Thursday with life threatening injuries after he was shot in a home on Century Oaks Lane, near where North Graham Street intersects Sugar Creek Road. Police were searching overnight for the other intruder. Police ask anyone with information to call 704-432-TIPS (8477) and speak directly to a homicide unit detective.
