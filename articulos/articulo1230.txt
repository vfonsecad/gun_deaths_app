Posted on: 9:18 pm, January 5, 2013, by Bliss Davis (Photo credit: MGN Online) CLEVELAND — A 911 call turned into a homicide investigation after a man found shot later died from his injuries on Saturday. Police received a shots fired call near 4067 E. 67th Street and Harvard Avenue at approximately 4:50 a.m. When they arrived on scene, they found a male victim with a single gunshot wound to the chest lying near a vehicle. The man, later identified as Brian Wilson, 41, was transported to MetroHealth Medical Center where he later died from his injuries. The Cleveland Division of Police Homicide Unit is investigating the shooting. Anyone with information regarding the incident should contact police immediately.
