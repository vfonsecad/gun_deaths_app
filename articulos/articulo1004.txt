1 Search 56° Partly Cloudy by Andrew Michalscheck azfamily.com Posted on December 16, 2012 at 10:52 AM           Updated          Wednesday, Dec 19 at 11:40 AM       View larger map                                PHOENIX -- Phoenix Police are searching for the person responsible for killing a 60-year-old man on Saturday afternoon. Authorities responded to the area of 23rd Avenue and Southern just after 12:30 p.m. and found the victim lying on the sidewalk. Police spoke with a witness who said she was inside her home when she heard a gunshot. The woman walked outside and heard another gunshot and some yelling. The woman then saw the victim lying on the sidewalk and saw a black male running from the area. Officers were unable to locate anyone after searching the area, and have only a vague description of the suspect.
