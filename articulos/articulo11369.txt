1 1  A man and his wife were found dead Thursday afternoon at a Tampa home, the Hillsborough County Sheriff’s Office said.  Michael Pardo, 71, and his wife, Geraldine Pardo, 70, were found dead inside a home at 1719 Warrington Way.  Deputies responded to the home just before 2 p.m. after receiving a panic alarm call that originated from the home, the sheriff’s office said.  Investigators found the two victims in the home. A firearm was also found, deputies said.  The case remains under investigation. Deputies aren’t searching for a suspect, the sheriff’s office said.  Stay with TBO.com for updates.
