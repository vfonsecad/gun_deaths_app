Deputies were called to a home around 10 a.m. Upon arrival, they found George Howard Moses, 49, dead from a gunshot to the head. Deputies received a call around 10:24 a.m. of a report of an armed robbery at Wells Fargo Bank, 1899 W. U.S. Highway 421. Wilkesboro police said the robber matched a description of the gunman in the shooting.  A North Carolina Highway Patrol trooper with Watauga County Sheri stopped the man suspected in the shooting in a white GMC pickup truck just east of Wilkesboro. The man was arrested at the North Pine Run Road intersection with U.S. 421.  Rainer Johannes Hoedl, 51, was arrested around 12:10 p.m.  Hoedl is in custody of the Wilkes County Sheriff's Office on charges of murder and robbery with a dangerous weapon. No bond has been set at this time.
