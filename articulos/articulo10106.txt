1 1 (credit: CBS) MINNEAPOLIS, Minn. – A Richfield man who died from multiple gunshot wounds in Minneapolis last week has been identified.  Thirty-one-year-old Juan Reyes Rosario was shot Monday night at 43rd Street East between South 4th and 5th avenues. Minneapolis Police think Reyes Rosario was targeted, and they are asking for help finding the driver of a car that might be involved in the deadly shooting. Police are looking for a Mercury Grand Marquis or Ford Crown Victoria with a blue paint job. The car does not have hub caps. If you have any information, please call police.
