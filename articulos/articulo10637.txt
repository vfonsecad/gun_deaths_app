1 1 Police are investigating the death of a man found shot in an alleyway behind a North Little Rock bar early Saturday morning. Just after 1 AM, officers arrived at Guanacos Billiards on the 3400 block of Pike Avenue and found 20-year-old Louis Oswaldo suffering from a gun-shot wound.   Oswaldo was taken to a local hospital, where he was pronounced dead.   Detectives are trying to find any witnesses who were around when the shooting happened.   No arrests have been made in this incident.
