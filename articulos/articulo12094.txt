1 1 A 38-year-old man who was shot in the head Monday in the city's Grand Crossing neighborhood has died, officials said. Tavache Kizer of the 7600 block of South Phillips Avenue was pronounced dead at 12:05 p.m. at Northwestern Memorial Hospital, according to a spokesman for the Cook County medical examiner's office. Kizer was shot about 3:45 p.m. Monday on the 7300 block of South Dorchester Avenue, said police. He was taken in critical condition to the hospital where he was pronounced dead this afternoon. Police said Monday that two people approached Kizer and shot him. They fled north on Dorchester Avenue. As of 7:50 p.m. Tuesday, no one was in custody for the attack.
