Crime & Justice More Links Dec 27, 2012 08:02 PM EST A man was found dead from an apparent gunshot wound in Capitol Heights on Thursday morning, Prince George’s County police said. Officers responded to a scene near Pepper Mill Drive and Drylog Street shortly before 7 a.m. for a report of a suspicious vehicle, police said.  They found Nicholas Andrew Battle, 21, of the 100 block of West Mill Road in Capitol Heights, suffering from a gunshot wound. He was pronounced dead at the scene, police said.  Police did not immediately disclose a suspect or motive.  This item has been updated.
