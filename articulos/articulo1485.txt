1   By Daniel M. Jimenez Bay Area News Group EMERYVILLE -- Police are investigating a rare homicide in this city after a man was found shot and killed Friday night. Police responded to the 1300 block of 65th Street, where they found the adult male victim, according to a police statement. His identity was not released pending notification of next of kin. Detectives are investigating the shooting, believed to be only the second slaying this year in the city. Police did not release any information on suspects or motive.
