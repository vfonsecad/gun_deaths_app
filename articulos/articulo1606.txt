The body of a 59-year-old Palm Desert woman who shot herself was found Tuesday on a Palm Desert golf course, coroner's and sheriff's officials said.Someone reported finding the woman, identified by Riverside County Coroners as Linda Carter of Palm Desert, around 7:45 a.m. at the Palm Desert Country Club, about 30 feet south of 77029 Utah Circle. Sheriff's Cpl. Angel Ramos said she used a handgun."For some unknown reason, she just decided to commit suicide on the golf course," he said.The coroner's office was working to pinpoint the time of death.
