1    A woman who was fatally shot early Thursday in southeast Denver has been identified as 58-year-old Deborah Hubbard, the medical examiner said Friday.   Officers found her with gunshot wounds when they arrived at an apartment complex at 10150 E. Virginia Ave. just before 6 a.m. They also found a man with multiple stab wounds, Denver police have said. Both were taken to Denver Health Medical Center, where Hubbard was pronounced dead. The medical examiner's office said she died of multiple gunshot wounds.
