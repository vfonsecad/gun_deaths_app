1 1 Police have identified a man killed outside Up or On the Rocks as 19-year-old Brian Simpe of Manchester. Simpe was shot once outside the popular club and died at an area hospital a short time later.  Police said Simpe was transported to Saint Francis Hospital and was pronounced dead at 2:22 a.m.  Witnesses to the shooting tell Eyewitness News that a scuffle broke out in the parking lot prior to the shooting. Detectives are investigating the shooting and are asking anyone with information to contact them.  Anyone with information is asked to call Lt.
