1                                                                                  	                                         															By ALYSSA DURANTY                                         												 / ORANGE COUNTY REGISTER 																						                                          PLACENTIA – A man was found dead with multiple gunshot wounds a residential alley early Sunday morning, police said. Police said they responded to the 600 block of Center Street at 5:20 a.m. after receiving multiple 911 calls. When officers arrived, they said they found a man with multiple gunshot wounds and began CPR. The man was declared dead at the scene, authorities said. Placentia detectives are investigating the death as a gang-related homicide, police Lt. Eric Point said. Officials said the identity of the man has not been released because the family has not been notified. Police currently do not have any witnesses or suspects. Anyone with information can contact Placentia police detectives at 714-993-8187 or can provide an anonymous tip to the Orange County Crime Stoppers at 855-TIP-OCCS.
