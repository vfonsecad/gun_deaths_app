1 1 HAMPTON A 55-year-old Hampton woman was found shot early this morning, police said. The woman was found about 4:40 a.m., in the 900 block of North King St., a police news release said. She was taken to a hospital with critical injuries from a gunshot wound to the upper torso.  The woman was still in the hospital as of 11 a.m. Police did not offer any suspect or motive information.
