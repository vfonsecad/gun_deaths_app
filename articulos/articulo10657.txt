The man has not been identified pending notification of next of kin, according to the San Bernardino County Sheriff-Coroner's website.  Police were called to the 4200 block of Kingsley Street at about 5:35 p.m. Saturday after receiving reports of shots being fired in the area.  They found a man inside a vehicle with gunshot wounds to the upper body, according to a the coroner's website. The man was pronounced dead at the scene.     Witnesses told police the gunman was a Latino man wearing a blue, long sleeved shirt.  Police detectives along with members of the San Bernardino County Sheriff's Department are investigating the incident.  Those with information on this case are asked to call the Police Department at 909-621-4771.
