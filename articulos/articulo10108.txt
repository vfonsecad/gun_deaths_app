1 1 (WMC-TV) - Police in Jackson, TN are investigating the shooting death of 19-year-old man.   Darvin Manning was found on the ground outside of his apartment with gunshot wounds.  He was taken to the Jackson-Madison County General Hospital where he was pronounced dead. Investigators believe Manning had just arrived at his apartment on Tinker Hill Cove when he was shot in the parking lot near his car.
