1 1   			 52° F 12:09 p.m. EDT, August 13, 2013 A man was killed in a shooting in North Baltimore early Tuesday morning, according to Baltimore Police. Tuesday afternoon, police identified the victim as Shawn Pooler, 22, of the 500 block of Harwood Avenue.  Officers responded to the 500 block of Tunbridge Road in the city's Mid-Govans neighborhood about 1:44 a.m. and located Pooler, police said. Homicide detectives are investigating, police said.
