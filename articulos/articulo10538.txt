1 1    A 31-year-old Hayward man has been identified as the victim of an apparent self-inflicted gunshot wound that was suffered at Target Masters West in Milpitas last week, the Santa Clara County Medical Examiner-Coroner's Office confirmed.  On Aug. 13 at approximately 9:29 p.m., Milpitas Police Department received a 911 call from an employee of Target Masters at 122 Minnis Circle, a business adjacent to the police department, reporting a person had been shot within one of the shooting bays, police said.   Several Milpitas police officers arrived on scene within a minute and located the victim, Dewayne Harvey Jr., suffering from a gunshot wound. Harvey still had vital signs and was transported by paramedics to Regional Medical Center of San Jose. At about 6:10 a.m. the next morning, he was pronounced deceased, police said.   On Monday, the coroner's office confirmed the victim died as a result of an intra-oral gunshot wound.   No one else was injured in the shooting.
